About Us

We are a private limited liability agric seeds producing and marketing company engaged in the business of agriculture cultivation, production, procurement, processing and marketing of agro seedlings and farm products. We also train farmers on the use of modern agricultural implements and the farming of high yielding seedling, as well as the management of farms for improved profitability.

Our Vision

To turn our fallow lands in Nigeria and Africa into bread, unlocking its seed treasures.

Our Mission

We exists to build a food secured Nigeria/Africa through setting up seed farms, trainings, and supporting local farmers through extension services.
